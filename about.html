About Us

Who We Are

Blossom & Books Bookkeeping is committed to providing thorough, reliable, and personalised bookkeeping solutions to businesses across Launceston and beyond. We focus on creating clarity in your financial operations, helping you make informed decisions and grow your business with confidence. From startups to established enterprises, our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of each client.

Our Approach

  • Meticulous accuracy: Every transaction is recorded and reconciled with precision, ensuring compliance and financial clarity.
  • Tailored services: We design bookkeeping processes that suit the specific requirements of your business.
  • Support you can trust: Friendly, approachable, and responsive assistance whenever you need it.

Meet Julie

Julie is the heart and mind behind Blossom & Books Bookkeeping. With extensive experience in bookkeeping and taxation compliance, she brings a wealth of knowledge and practical expertise to every client. As a registered BAS Agent with the Tax Practitioners Board and a certified member of the Institute of Certified Bookkeepers, Julie ensures all financial records are accurate, up-to-date, and compliant with Australian taxation requirements.

Julie's approach goes beyond standard bookkeeping. She works closely with clients to understand their business operations, providing actionable insights and tailored advice that support growth and operational efficiency. Her services include:

  • Comprehensive bookkeeping and transaction management
  • Payroll setup and timely salary processing
  • Tax compliance and BAS preparation
  • Financial reporting and advisory guidance

Outside of bookkeeping, Julie is passionate about empowering small business owners. She understands the challenges of running a business firsthand and enjoys helping entrepreneurs gain confidence in their financial decisions. Her dedication to integrity, accuracy, and personalised service makes her not just a bookkeeper, but a trusted advisor and partner in business growth.
