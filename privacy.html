1. Introduction

At Blossom & Books Bookkeeping, we are committed to protecting your privacy and the confidentiality of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). By using our website or engaging our services, you agree to this policy.

2. What Personal Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, business name, ABN, financial data, and details necessary to provide bookkeeping or BAS services. We only collect sensitive information with your consent or where required by law.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information when you contact us, complete online forms, engage our services, or provide access to accounting platforms like Xero or MYOB. We may also collect website analytics data to improve our services.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Deliver bookkeeping, BAS, and payroll services
  • Communicate with you regarding your accounts or our services
  • Issue invoices, statements, or compliance reports
  • Meet our obligations as a registered BAS Agent
  • Improve our website and client experience

5. Disclosure of Personal Information

We may share information with the Australian Taxation Office (ATO), accounting software providers (e.g., Xero, MYOB), professional advisors, or third-party service providers who support our business. We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data from misuse, loss, or unauthorised access, including encryption, password protection, and limited access to authorised staff. In the event of a data breach, we will act in accordance with the Notifiable Data Breaches Scheme.

7. Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us via email. We may verify your identity before providing access.

8. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to improve performance and user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

9. Retention of Information

We retain records as required by law and securely destroy them when no longer needed.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
